Knowingnone

Knowingnone, the 14-year-old creative alias of Aidan H, blends complex rhythms and diverse textures in his rapidly evolving soundscapes. Starting with drumming at age three and producing by ten, his music defies genre boundaries with fluid, experimental journeys. Known for pushing sound design limits, he captivates listeners with intricate arrangements and unconventional rhythms. Despite his age, Knowingnone is carving a unique path in electronic music, promising a bold future ahead.

HC76

HC76, a 14-year-old artist anonymously named, crafts ambient electronic music that evokes calm and reflection. His music is characterized by soothing, atmospheric soundscapes and subtle, minimalistic rhythms. HC76’s ability to blend intricate textures with a peaceful, introspective vibe sets his work apart, showing a maturity beyond his years. As he continues to develop his sound, HC76 is emerging as a notable figure in the ambient music scene, offering listeners a tranquil escape through his compositions.

TrashPasta

TrashPasta, a 15-year-old artist, brings high-energy tracks that blend elements of rap, hip-hop, and experimental sounds. Known for his fast-paced flows, glitchy beats, and distorted vocals, his music creates an intense and raw vibe. TrashPasta’s lyrics explore themes of rebellion, self-expression, and breaking free from conventions. With his unique sound and delivery, he's carving out a space in the rap scene, offering a bold and unpredictable voice that stands out in modern hip-hop.

Big Matt & The Producer

Big Matt & The Producer is a rap duo that speaks directly to the struggles of life in tough environments. Big Matt, a 14-year-old from the Bronx, pours his heart into his lyrics, confronting the hardships of financial and housing struggles with raw honesty. The Producer, Knowingnone, elevates Big Matt’s introspective storytelling with tough, street-level rhythms that capture the grit and intensity of their experiences. Together, they create a sound that connects on a deeper level, blending social commentary with bold, urban beats.
